AL-THURAYA UNITED EST is a Leading company dealing in Personal Protective Equipment, Industrial Safety Equipment, Fire Extinguishers & Hydrants, Our product range includes Personal Protective Equipment like Safety Helmets, Safety Eyewear, Hearing Protective Equipment, Protective Face Shields, Respiratory Protective Equipment, Industrial Hand Gloves, Industrial Garments, Safety Shoes, Gas Detectors, Spill Kits & Sorbents, Traffic Safety Products, Fire Extinguishers & Hydrants, and a vast range of Fall Protection Equipment. Besides these, engineered safety solutions like anchorage systems, specialized rope access, evacuation and rescue products, and confined space entry/ egress systems also form a part of our Product Portfolio. We also provide services like, Refilling of SCBA Cylinders and maintenance of Fire Extinguishers. “AL‐THURAYA" has got the recognition in the field of Supply of Industrial Safety & Personal protective equipment and Fire Extinguishers & Hydrants, just because of ‐  Complete range of Quality products confirming to International standards & after sales support from our principles.  Eagerness to deliver best possible services & timely supply to our esteemed customers.  Extended services to site safety surveys that help prescribing an effective safety equipment mix and live demonstrations, trainings & workshops to ensure effective equipment utilization.  Well‐equipped state‐of‐art technology, excellent infrastructure and qualified & trained staff. We provide complete safety solution across the Automobile & Ancillary, Construction, Engineering, Oil & Gas, Cement, Fire Services, Pharmaceuticals, Power, Petrochemicals, Steel, Transmission, Telecommunication and General Industries. We strongly believe in providing the best possible & comfortable safety solution to minimize the accidents at your workplace & maximize the protection of your workforce.
